This page will define all the available roles connected to your club, and point you to guides on the tasks for your role.

## Club referent

A club referent is essentially the main administrative manager of a club. They will have access to everything club related.

- To register players, check out the guide on [player registration](/registrations/players)
- To register coaches or other club officials, check out the guide on [registering team officials](/registrations/team-officials)
- To add or modify club uniforms, check out the guide on [club uniforms](/club-operations/club-uniforms)
